Who we are?

In 2025, C.C.C.S.E.L. was founded, a non-profit organization by three friends who met while studying the Microcomputer and Network Systems (SMX) training program. Over the years, they shared not only friendship but also close experiences with the world of addictions: gambling, drugs, and alcoholism, whether in their immediate surroundings or in their own skin. Aware of the stigma and silence that often surround these issues, they decided to create a space for awareness, prevention, and real support for those who need it most. Thus, C.C.C.S.E.L. was born, with the aim of building bridges where there were once walls and offering a second chance to those who believe they have lost everything. From talks at schools to social media campaigns, workshops, emotional support, and collaboration with professionals, our work is based on empathy, closeness, and lived experience. Because we understand the problem from the inside, and because we firmly believe that any life can be put back on track with support and dignity. C.C.C.S.E.L. represents that: a new opportunity, a step forward, a restart. Because starting over isn't giving up. It's resisting with hope.
