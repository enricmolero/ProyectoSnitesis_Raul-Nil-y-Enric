What we do on C.C.C.S.E.L

At C.C.C.S.E.L., we are dedicated to providing comprehensive support to people struggling with gambling and substance addiction, focusing on awareness and recovery through individualized therapy, mindfulness talks, peer support groups, and dynamic outdoor activities. Our goal is to offer a safe, supportive, and motivating environment that encourages reflection, emotional empowerment, and the adoption of healthy lifestyle habits. We firmly believe in the power of human support, active listening, and community work to help each person regain control of their lives and build a future free of addiction.
